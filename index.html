Two Archives, One Story

The mentoring archive is organized into two collections. Faculty members document their mentoring activities — research projects, conference presentations, and the lasting impact on student development. Students reflect on transformative experiences in light of the BYU Aims of a university education.

About This Archive

This archive was created to help the BYU Department of Linguistics preserve, surface, and share the work of faculty mentors and the transformative experiences of students. It serves the department's reporting needs, supports retention and promotion documentation, and tells a richer story about the kind of mentoring that distinguishes a BYU education.

Entries can be public or private at the contributor's discretion. Public entries appear in the open archive; private content is reserved for departmental review.
