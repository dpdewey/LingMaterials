Submit an Entry

Record a mentoring moment worth keeping.

Faculty across Linguistics, TESOL, and Editing & Publishing shape students through projects, courses, and study-abroad experiences. Use this form to document the work, the students, and what made it meaningful.

i. The Mentor

Faculty member, instructor, or other mentor recording this entry.

ii. The Venue

Title and a few words about the project, course, or experience.

iii. The Mentees

List every student involved, separated by commas.

iv. Accomplishments

Conference presentations, publications, awards, completed projects, skills mastered, languages learned, communities served.

v. Mentor Reflection

What are you proud of? What key moments stand out? How did this experience feel? Speak freely — this is the heart of the archive.

vi. Personal Impact

What impact did this experience or a specific student have on your life — your approach to teaching, your desire to mentor more students this way, your personal testimony, your appetite for learning?

vii. Image — optional

A picture of the mentees, the venue, fieldwork, a poster, an editing manuscript — anything that captures the experience. JPG or PNG, up to 4 MB.

This page is private and not indexed by search engines. Submissions appear on the public archive once added.
