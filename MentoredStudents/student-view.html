What it felt like to be mentored.

Mentoring here is a partnership focused on your development as a whole person. Faculty act as stewards of your success — treating you as a junior colleague, modeling how to balance professional life with deep personal faith, and helping you discover what you are capable of through hands-on, real-world work. These are the moments students remember.
